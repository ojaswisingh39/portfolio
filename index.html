About Me

My Journey

Over the past 5 months, I worked as a UI/UX Designer at a software company, where I was involved in creating intuitive and user-friendly UI designs for web-based applications. My responsibilities included designing wireframes, high-fidelity mockups, and interactive prototypes to support development teams.

I also contributed to a government mobile app project by designing the home page UI tailored for mobile users. Additionally, I created festival-themed graphics and regular social media posts for an online bookstore, focusing on engaging Facebook content and visually appealing brand designs.

My Paintings

I create unique and expressive paintings that capture emotions and stories. My work spans various styles and techniques, from abstract to realistic, always exploring new ways to express my artistic vision.

Each painting is a journey, a reflection of my experiences and observations of the world around me. I work primarily with acrylics and oils, but I also experiment with mixed media to create texture and depth.
